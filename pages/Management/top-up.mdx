# Add money to your card

All deposits get mixed automatically on deposit. 

To begin 
1. open the Main Menu on the @TeleCard_Robot
2. Enter desired amount to top-up 0.04 minimum. Else there is $5 fee for top-ups below $50.
3. Send exactly the same or more to the address given by the bot
4. Your deposit will complete automatically and the card balance will get updated

[Check out this video!](https://files-hosdt.vercel.app/tut6.mov)

> Allow up to 20 minutes for the funds to apply to your card.

# What is TeleCard?

TeleCard bridges the gap between FIAT and CRYPTO so you can spend anywhere in the world. It's the quickest and the most secure way to load your crypto assets on to a VISA or MasterCard.

# How does it work?

TeleCard is a platform that allows you to convert your crypto assets into FIAT and load them onto a VISA or MasterCard. You can then use your card to make purchases anywhere in the world.
